Offset Printing

Offset printing is a commonly used traditional printing technique where requirement is in bulk. Inks and rollers are used to edge and curve the product. Its implied in Booklets making, pamphelets etc.

Digital Printing

Digital printing is a direct printing technique from a digital-based image. A few applications are Desktop publishing, advertising and event signage, in number of sectors at point of sale or purchase.

Post Press

Every product has a cutting edge stage to provide finishing. Post press activities involves Cutting , Binding, Stitching of books, Assembling, Folding of brochures, Embossing and Debossing.
